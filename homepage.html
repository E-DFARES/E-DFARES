Computer Maintenance & Complaint System

The Computer Maintenance & Complaint System at Kolej Kemahiran Johor Bahru (KKJ JB) is a platform developed to facilitate students and staff in submitting complaints related to technical issues and computer maintenance at the college. This system aims to streamline the reporting process for technical problems such as computer malfunctions, network issues, software failures, or other concerns related to IT equipment.

With this system, users can submit complaints online, where each issue is recorded, tracked, and managed by the responsible technical team. The system also allows users to monitor the status of their complaints in real-time and receive prompt feedback from the technicians.
